수면 무호흡증(OSA)이란

Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) is a condition where breathing is temporarily paused or becomes shallow during sleep due to the partial or complete obstruction or narrowing of the upper airway. This leads to restricted airflow and difficulty in breathing, resulting in reduced sleep quality and efficiency. OSA encompasses both the transient cessation of breathing, known as "apnea," and the functional hemodynamic changes that follow periods of apnea.
